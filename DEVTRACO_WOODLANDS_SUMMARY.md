## Development Rules & Regulations

### Construction Requirements
- Full development must be completed within **5 years** of plot delivery
- All construction must comply with Development, Monitoring, and Control Committee (DMCC) guidelines
- Approval required before starting any building or modification
- Modifications require an Alteration Permit (valid 6 months)

### Community Standards
- All designs must align with estate architectural standards
- Pets allowed: Cats and dogs only (must not disturb neighbors)
- Trash disposal: Specific containers required
- No personal items on common property or roads
- Property transfer requires written consent from sub-lessor
- No commercial use (renting/subletting for business) without consent
