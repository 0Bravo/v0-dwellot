## 1. CUSTOMER SEGMENTS 👥

### Primary Segments

**A. Property Seekers**
- **Diaspora/Expats** (25-45 years)
  - Ghanaians abroad looking to invest back home
  - International professionals relocating to Ghana
  - High purchasing power, seek verified listings
  
- **Urban Professionals** (28-50 years)
  - Middle-upper class Ghanaians
  - Based in Accra, Kumasi, Tema
  - Looking for quality homes in premium areas
  
- **First-Time Buyers** (25-35 years)
  - Young professionals
  - Price-sensitive, need financing options
  - Seeking affordable entry points (like Devtraco Jute)

- **Property Investors** (35-60 years)
  - Looking for land/plots for development
  - Interested in rental income properties
  - ROI-focused decision makers

**B. Property Providers**
- **Real Estate Developers** 
  - Appolonia City (13 properties) - Premium mixed-use development
  - Devtraco Group (2 properties) - Devtraco Woodlands gated city
  - BestWorld Company/Kharis Properties (3 properties) - Quality residential
- **Individual Property Owners**
- **Real Estate Agents/Brokers**

### Market Size (Ghana)
- Population: 33M+ (6M+ in Greater Accra)
- Middle class growing at 8-10% annually
- Diaspora remittances: $4B+ annually
- Housing deficit: 1.8M+ units

## 5. REVENUE STREAMS 💰

### Current Revenue Model

**Commission-Based (Primary)**
- **Listing Fees** from developers/agents
  - Featured placement fees
  - Premium listing upgrades
  
- **Transaction Commission** (2-5% of property value)
  - Paid by developer/seller upon successful sale
  - Standard industry practice in Ghana

## RISK ANALYSIS ⚠️

### Key Risks & Mitigation

**Market Risks**
- Risk: Economic downturn affects property sales
- Mitigation: Diversify revenue (rentals, consultancy), focus on diaspora

**Competition Risks**
- Risk: Established players (Meqasa, Tonaton) increase investment
- Mitigation: Focus on premium segment, superior UX, exclusive partnerships

**Technology Risks**
- Risk: Platform downtime, security breaches
- Mitigation: Regular backups, security audits, $250-500/month maintenance

**Partnership Risks**
- Risk: Developer partners list elsewhere or direct
- Mitigation: Exclusive listing agreements, value-added services

**Regulatory Risks**
- Risk: New real estate regulations in Ghana
- Mitigation: Stay compliant, join industry associations
